The Mercy That Follows

Author: Nova Storm

When the Veil between worlds tears open, Aeryn—a streetwise survivor with a stolen heart—finds herself bound to Kaden, a brooding exiled prince whose touch ignites a power she can’t control. Hunted by the dark court that seeks to claim her gift, Aeryn must navigate treacherous alliances, forbidden magic, and a passion that threatens to consume them both.

In a world where trust is a rare currency and loyalty is bought in blood, Aeryn and Kaden must decide what they’re willing to sacrifice—each other, or the destiny that might save them both.

A sweeping fantasy romance where secrets unravel and hearts collide, perfect for fans of Sarah J. Maas and Leigh Bardugo.
